The Film version of the musical "Hair", starring Ellen Foley,

and a whole lotta other people.

"Hair", 1979, 121 minutes, colour.

The film "Hair" is basically the songs from the musical, filmed in various locations.



.
Approximately 69 mins 50 secs into the film, Ellen's one and only musical number appears. Duration 1 min 15 secs, "Black Boys", features Ellen Foley, singing and dancing, and is presumably set in New York's Central Park.


The film also stars John Savage, Treat Williams, Beverly D'Angelo, Annie Golden, Don Dacus and Cheryl Barnes.
